# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 27, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Colour Me Brazil ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information from children under the age of 13, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD).

## COPPA Compliance Statement

Colour Me Brazil is designed for children ages 6-12. We comply with COPPA by:
- Obtaining verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13
- Providing parents with notice of our information practices
- Allowing parents to review, delete, and control the collection of their child's information
- Limiting the collection of personal information to what is reasonably necessary for participation
- Protecting the confidentiality, security, and integrity of personal information collected from children

## Information We Collect

### From Children (with Parental Consent):
- **Profile Information:** First name, age, preferred language, avatar selection
- **Usage Data:** Reading progress, books completed, coloring activities, points earned
- **Created Content:** Colored artwork, forum posts, collaborative stories (moderated)
- **Device Information:** Browser type, device type, IP address (for security purposes)

### From Parents/Guardians:
- **Contact Information:** Email address for account verification and communication
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
- **Consent Records:** Documentation of parental consent and preferences

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and personalize the educational experience
- Track learning progress and award achievements
- Enable social features (forum, showcase) with moderation
- Send important updates to parents about their child's account
- Improve our platform and develop new features
- Ensure platform safety and security
- Comply with legal obligations

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share children's personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information with:
- **Service Providers:** Base44 (backend services), Stripe (payment processing) - all COPPA compliant
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect safety and rights
- **With Parental Consent:** Only when explicitly authorized by the parent

## Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:
- **Review** their child's personal information
- **Request deletion** of their child's personal information
- **Refuse further collection** or use of their child's information
- **Modify** privacy settings and communication preferences

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@colourmebrazil.com

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication via Base44 SDK
- Regular security audits and updates
- Role-based access controls
- Data minimization practices

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Automatically deleted after 24 months of inactivity
- **Deletion Requests:** Processed within 30 days
- **Backup Copies:** Securely deleted within 90 days

## Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to:
- Maintain login sessions
- Remember user preferences (language, theme)
- Analyze site performance (anonymized data only)

**We do NOT use cookies for:**
- Behavioral advertising
- Third-party tracking
- Cross-site data collection

Parents can manage cookie preferences in browser settings.

## International Data Transfers

Our services are hosted on secure servers. If data is transferred internationally, we ensure adequate safeguards through:
- Standard contractual clauses
- GDPR and LGPD compliance measures
- Encryption during transfer

## GDPR Rights (EU Users)

EU residents have additional rights:
- **Right to Access:** Request copies of personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** "Right to be forgotten"
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how data is used
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive data in portable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain processing activities

## LGPD Rights (Brazilian Users)

Brazilian users have rights including:
- Confirmation of data processing
- Access to personal data
- Correction of incomplete or inaccurate data
- Anonymization, blocking, or deletion of data
- Portability of data to another service provider
- Information about public and private entities with which data is shared

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify parents of material changes via:
- Email notification to registered parent accounts
- Prominent notice on our website
- Re-obtaining parental consent when required
