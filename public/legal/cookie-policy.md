## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the service more useful.

## Cookies We Use

### 1. **Essential Cookies** (Required)
These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `session_token` | Maintains user login session | Session | First-party |
| `currentProfileId` | Remembers selected child profile | Persistent | First-party |
| `base44_auth` | Authentication with Base44 backend | Session | First-party |

**These cookies cannot be disabled** as they are required for core functionality.

### 2. **Preference Cookies** (Optional)
These cookies remember your choices and settings:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `language_preference` | Remembers chosen language (EN/PT) | 1 year | First-party |
| `theme_preference` | Saves light/dark mode choice | 1 year | First-party |
| `sidebar_state` | Remembers sidebar open/closed | 30 days | First-party |
| `reading_settings` | Stores reading preferences (font size, spacing) | 1 year | First-party |

### 3. **Analytics Cookies** (Optional, Anonymized)
We use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `_analytics_session` | Anonymous usage statistics | Session | First-party |

**Important:** 
- All analytics data is **anonymized**
- IP addresses are **masked**
- No personal identification possible
- Used only to improve the Service

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser:

**Chrome:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Privacy and Security ‚Üí Cookies and other site data
2. Choose "Block third-party cookies" or "Block all cookies"

**Firefox:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose "Delete cookies and site data when Firefox is closed"

**Safari:**
1. Preferences ‚Üí Privacy
2. Choose "Block all cookies"

**Edge:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage and delete cookies

### In-App Cookie Settings
Parents can manage optional cookies in **Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Cookie Preferences**.
