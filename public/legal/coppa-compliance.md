## Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Colour Me Brazil is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of children who use our platform. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), a United States federal law designed to protect the privacy of children under 13 years of age.

## What is COPPA?

COPPA requires websites and online services directed at children under 13 to:
1. Post a clear and comprehensive privacy policy
2. Provide notice and obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information
3. Give parents the choice to consent to collection and use of information
4. Provide parents access to their child's personal information
5. Give parents the opportunity to delete their child's personal information
6. Not condition a child's participation on disclosure of more information than is reasonably necessary
7. Maintain the confidentiality, security, and integrity of personal information
